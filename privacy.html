Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to protecting your data.

1. Information We Collect

FundBridge collects the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • Campaign Creators: Name, email address, wallet address, campaign details, and identification documents for verification
  • Donors: Name (optional), email (optional), donation amount, and payment information (processed by third-party gateways)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies, and usage patterns
  • Blockchain Data: Transaction hashes, wallet addresses, and donation amounts recorded on Polygon network

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process donations and campaign payouts securely
  • To verify campaign creators and prevent fraud
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements (AML/KYC)
  • To send notifications about campaigns and platform updates
  • To analyze platform performance and user behavior

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed through third-party gateways:

  • MoonPay: Handles crypto on-ramp and off-ramp transactions
  • Transak: Alternative payment processing with multiple payment methods
  • Ramp Network: Additional payment processing for global markets
Important: We do not store credit card or banking information on our servers. All sensitive payment data is handled directly by our payment partners.

4. Blockchain Data

All donations are recorded on the Polygon blockchain. This includes:

  • Donation amount in MATIC
  • Donor wallet address (or smart contract address for anonymous donations)
  • Campaign wallet address
  • Transaction timestamp
  • Transaction hash for verification
Note: Blockchain transactions are public and cannot be deleted or modified. Donors who wish to remain anonymous can use privacy-preserving wallets.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure HTTPS connections throughout the platform
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • Data encryption at rest for stored information
  • Continuous monitoring for suspicious activities

6. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Analytics and performance tracking
  • Remembering user preferences and settings
  • Improving website functionality

You can control cookie settings in your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some platform features.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to data processing for marketing purposes

Contact us at privacy@fundbridge.com for any data requests.
